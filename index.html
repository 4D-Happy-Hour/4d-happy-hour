4D Happy Hour is an independent weekly meeting place for 4D developers to talk shop, compare approaches, trade tips, and learn from each other’s real-world code.

It is informal, practical, and community-driven. No sales pitch. No official agenda required. Just experienced developers helping each other discover what is possible with 4D.

Not affiliated with 4D, Inc.

4D Happy Hour is an independent community gathering run by and for 4D developers.
