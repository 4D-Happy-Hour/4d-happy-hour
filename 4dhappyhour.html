About 4D Happy Hour

4D Happy Hour started in 2020 as a way for 4D developers to connect during the pandemic. What began as casual Friday gatherings has grown into a weekly tradition — a place to talk about the nerdy details of 4D, discuss the business side of being a 4D developer, network, and share code and demos.

These meetings have become known for live demos and screen sharing. Seeing how other developers work is always appreciated, and frequently someone with decades of experience will say "wow — I never thought of doing that."

What to expect

  • Casual format — come and go as you need
  • Typically runs about 2 hours, sometimes longer
  • We do not record meetings — this encourages open screen sharing
  • Bring your 4D problems and get expert suggestions
  • Everyone is welcome, from beginners to veterans

Want to demo something or present on a specific topic? Contact Kirk at kirk@everything4d.com to reserve a slot.
